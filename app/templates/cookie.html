What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that the site sends to the terminal of the interested party (usually the browser), on which they are stored and then sent back to the site once they are viewed a second time. Cookies can be used to record information about your preferences, such as browsed pages or downloading files from our site or other similar actions made by browsing our site.

First and third party cookies

Cookies, so called, of "first part" are the cookies developed by the owner of the site, while "third party" cookies are defined as cookies developed by third parties, that is, different from the owner of the site. This is because on each site may be present elements (images, maps, sounds, specific links to web pages of other domains, etc.) that reside on servers other than that of the site visited.

  • persistent cookies: these are used to store information, for example, the login name and password, so that the user does not have to type them again every time you visit a specific site. These also remain stored in the computer after you close your browser.
